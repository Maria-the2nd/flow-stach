Common Questions

Do I need to code?

+

Nope. Flow Bridge handles the technical translation. You just paste AI output and get Webflow-ready components. If you can copy-paste, you're qualified.

Does this replace Webflow?

+

No—it makes Webflow even better. Flow Bridge is the bridge between AI generation and Webflow's design system. You still polish in Webflow.

What AI tools do you support?

+

Anything that exports HTML/CSS/JS. Cursor, v0, Bolt, Claude artifacts, ChatGPT exports—if it's valid HTML, we work with it.

Will it be perfect every time?

+

Let's be real: no. AI output varies wildly. But we get you 80% there instantly. The last 20% is where your taste shines.
